# Geotab Brand Guidelines

## Tone of Voice
- Professional but approachable.
- Innovative yet grounded in reliability.
- Use active voice.
- Avoid jargon where possible.

## Forbidden Words
- "Revolutionary" (use "Advanced" or "Innovative")
- "Disruptive"
- "Cheap" (use "Cost-effective")
- "Killer feature"

## Formatting
- Use bullet points for lists.
- Headers should be sentence case.
- Geotab is always capitalized.

## Key Messaging
- Leading connected transportation solutions.
- Data-driven insights.
- Sustainability and safety focus.
