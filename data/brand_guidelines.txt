BRAND VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES

Company: Wealthsimple
Mission: Making powerful financial tools available to everyone.

Brand Voice:
- Simple and human — no financial jargon
- Honest and transparent (especially about fees)
- Encouraging, never condescending
- Proudly Canadian

Tone Guidelines:
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise (under 20 words)
- Address real financial anxieties directly
- Always include a clear, specific benefit (not vague)
- Avoid urgency tactics or FOMO language

Key Messaging:
- Commission-free investing for everyone
- High-interest savings, no hidden fees
- File your taxes free with Wealthsimple Tax
- Automated portfolios built by experts, managed by algorithms

Prohibited Terms:
- "Get rich quick" / "guaranteed returns" / "risk-free"
- "Revolutionary" (overused)
- "Game-changer" / "game changer" (cliché, prohibited)
- Competitor names: Questrade, TD Direct Investing, RBC Direct Investing, Nest Wealth, Betterment

Visual Identity: Wealthsimple Green — #000000 text on #ffffff background, accent #00d4a1, minimalist sans-serif
Logo: Always use full logo with wordmark; never icon alone
