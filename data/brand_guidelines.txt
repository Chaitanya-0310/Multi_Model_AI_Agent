BRAND VOICE & TONE GUIDELINES

Company: TechFlow Solutions
Mission: Empowering businesses through innovative cloud solutions

Brand Voice:
- Professional yet approachable
- Technical but not jargon-heavy
- Solution-focused and results-driven
- Innovative and forward-thinking

Tone Guidelines:
- Use active voice
- Keep sentences concise (under 25 words)
- Address pain points directly
- Always include measurable benefits
- Avoid superlatives like "best" or "perfect"

Key Messaging:
- Cloud migration made simple
- 50% faster deployment times
- Enterprise-grade security
- 24/7 customer support

Prohibited Terms:
- "Revolutionary" (overused)
- "Game-changer" (clich√©)
- Competitor names: CloudCorp, DataMax, SkyNet Solutions
