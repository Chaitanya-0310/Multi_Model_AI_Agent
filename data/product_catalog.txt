PRODUCT CATALOG - Wealthsimple

1. Wealthsimple Invest
   - Automated robo-advisor portfolio management
   - Global ETF diversification; socially responsible investing (SRI) option
   - Accounts: TFSA, RRSP, FHSA, non-registered, corporate
   - Fees: 0.5%/yr (Basic), 0.4%/yr (Premium $100k+), 0.4%/yr (Generation $500k+)
   - Target audience: First-time investors and hands-off wealth builders aged 25–40
   - Key stat: Portfolios designed using Nobel-prize-influenced research
   - Key features: Automatic rebalancing, dividend reinvestment, socially responsible portfolios

2. Wealthsimple Trade
   - Commission-free stock and ETF trading (TSX, NYSE, NASDAQ)
   - CAD & USD accounts; real-time quotes
   - Accounts: TFSA, RRSP, FHSA, personal, corporate
   - Pricing: Free (Basic); $10/month (Premium) — Level 2 market data, instant deposits, USD account
   - Target audience: Self-directed investors, millennials, active traders switching from bank brokerages
   - Key stat: Over 3 million Canadians use Wealthsimple
   - Key features: Fractional shares, no FX conversion on USD account, real-time Level 2 data (Premium)

3. Wealthsimple Cash
   - High-interest savings: 4.5% (Premium), 3.75% (Basic)
   - No-fee chequing account + Visa prepaid card
   - Instant e-Transfer; no minimum balance
   - CDIC eligible (deposits held at partner CDIC member institutions)
   - Target audience: Everyday Canadians, savers, those building an emergency fund or FHSA savings
   - Key features: Instant e-Transfers, no monthly fees, no minimum balance, joint accounts

4. Wealthsimple Tax
   - Free online tax filing (formerly SimpleTax)
   - Auto-fill from CRA My Account (NETFILE certified)
   - Supports: T4, T4A, RRSP receipts, T5, investment income, self-employment
   - Pay-what-you-want model
   - Target audience: All Canadians filing personal tax returns, especially first-time filers and RRSP/TFSA contributors
   - Key features: CRA auto-fill, NETFILE certified, guides for capital gains and RRSP deductions
