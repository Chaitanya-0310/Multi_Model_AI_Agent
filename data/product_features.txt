# Product: Geotab GO9

## Overview
The Geotab GO9 is our most advanced telematics device.

## Features
- Global connectivity (LTE-M).
- Intelligent accident detection and notification.
- Engine data reporting (RPM, fuel usage, coolant temperature).
- Driver behavior monitoring (harsh braking, cornering, speeding).
- IOX expandability for add-ons (cameras, temperature sensors).

## Use Cases
- Fleet management optimization.
- Regulatory compliance (ELD).
- Electric vehicle suitability assessments.
- Sustainability reporting.

## Target Audience
- Fleet Managers.
- Operations Directors.
- Safety Officers.
- Sustainability Leads.
